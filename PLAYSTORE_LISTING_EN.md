## Full Description (4000 chars max)
**Feel the laws of the universe at your fingertips!**

Visual Science Lab helps you understand complex science and math concepts through interactive simulations that bring learning to life.

📚 **430+ Interactive Simulations**
• Physics: Pendulum, Double Pendulum, Projectile Motion, Spring Oscillation, Electric Fields, Wave Interference, Gravity
• Math: Function Graphs, Quadratic Functions, Vectors, Fourier Transform, Mandelbrot Set, 3D Surfaces
• Chaos Theory: Lorenz Attractor, Three-Body Problem, Logistic Map
• AI/ML: Neural Networks, Gradient Descent, K-Means, SVM, Decision Trees
• Chemistry: Bohr Model, Molecular Orbitals, Chemical Reactions
• Quantum: Bloch Sphere, Qubit States, Wave Functions
• Astronomy: Planetary Orbits, Solar System, Stellar Evolution
• Biology: Cell Division, Population Dynamics, DNA Structure
• And many more across 14 categories!

🎮 **Learn by Doing**
• Adjust parameters in real-time and observe changes instantly
• Multiple presets and initial conditions to explore
• Formulas and explanations alongside each simulation

🤖 **AI-Powered Learning**
• AI Chatbot: Ask questions about any simulation
• AI Explanations: Get clear breakdowns at 4 difficulty levels (middle school → university)
• Powered by GPT-4o-mini for accurate, helpful responses

🌟 **Key Features**
• Beautiful dark theme UI designed for focus
• Category filtering and search
• Learning progress tracking
• Favorites for quick access
• Works offline — no internet required for simulations
• Supports English and Korean

🔄 **Constantly Updated**
New simulations and features added regularly!

🌐 **Web Version**
https://3dweb-rust.vercel.app

Perfect for middle school, high school, and university students, as well as anyone curious about science.
